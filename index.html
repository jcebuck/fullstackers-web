Why to hire us?

Before making a decision on what company to hire, just read below!

Documentation

Make sure you get all you need and nothing else! By reading this document you will know exactly what it's included in the service hired, how much it's gonna cost you and when it's gonna be done.

15% Money Back

Not happy with the final outcome? Just email us explaining why exacly your're not happy and we'll refund you 15% of our services! Note: This is only valid for the first 7 days after your full payment.

Flexible Payments

Cashflow isn't just much in the first few months! Tell us about your needs and we might be able to offer your flexible payment conditions and you get your website up and running before paying us totally!

Our Pricing?

Just power-off some wrong ideas you might have in mind! Every project is unique and we'll charge you accordingly to the services hired and nothing else. Guess what? We may cost less than you think! Dare to ask us how much it would cost you to build up what you have in mind!

Commission Based Services

We believe so much that we can build you up a nice e-commerce store that we'll do it for free. Yes, you only pay us a set commission (%) everytime you sell something via the website! Selling more than expected? So buy the e-store from us and stop paying us commissions since that day!
